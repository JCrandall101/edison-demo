Beyond the Grid Fund for Zambia (BGFZ) is a Sida-funded initiative for renewable energy investments outside the national power grid in Zambia. BGFZ is designed accelerate market access and development, promoting the development of energy services targeted to energy-poor consumers. The overall goal of the BGFZ is to facilitate the access to modern energy of at least one million Zambians.

EDISON collects data from Energy Service Providers in Zambia to provide visailibty into the BFGZ program. EDISON provides a number of dashboards to allow users to interact with the data and visualize the progress of providing energy to Zambia.
